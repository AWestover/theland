Information about the game

The currency in this game is "the knight" If you get knights you can buy an animal animals will grow and reproduce (and sometimes get sick and die as dictated by the cycle of life) the goal of the game is to get the most animals good luck Move arround the board by scrolling with your finger or mouse attack other players in order to harm them and get knights for defeating them

How does the currency work?

You have a somewhat steady (yet slightly random) constant income source Click on the buy button to get an animal in the current center of your screen this costs 100 knights

Winning and losing

If you run out of animals you lose. If you have the most animals your are in the lead! see the scoreboard by pressing Toggle Scores for your current standing
