The Land

Information about the game

The goal of the game is to get the most animals Move arround the board by scrolling with your finger or mouse or tilting your moblie device if it has an acceleromter attack other players in order to harm them and get stormlight for defeating them

Currency

The currency in this game is "stormlight" If you get stormlight you can buy an animal. You can't buy any more once you are at 15 animals, but they may reproduce still. (as of now you cannot manipulate gravity once you attain stormlight. This feature would be cool, but probably is not feasible to add without support from NASA) animals will grow and reproduce (and sometimes get sick and die as dictated by the cycle of life)

Age

This is how old your animal is.

Strength

This is how much damage you do.

Hunger

If you get too hungry you will die. Feed your animals!

Speed

Faster animals move faster...

User stats

You chose a name, a world and some other stuff too. Sorry but I don't allow names longer than 15 characters they will be truncated if they are too long also world names are subject to this constraint

How does the currency work?

You have a somewhat steady (yet slightly random) constant income source Click on the buy button to get an animal in the current center of your screen this costs 100 stormlight for now

Winning and losing

Look at the leaderboard to see your score your score is determined mostly by how many animals you are fielding but other factors like stormlight contribute to your score as well see the scoreboard by pressing Toggle Scores for your current standing.

Food

Eat food. It is good for you

Predators

These will eat YOU. They will also destroy food, but they can only eat humans. If you eat one you get boosted, which on the first time it happens will double your health

Toggle Attract

your animals may be attracted to the center if you want which gives a higher chance of level up, but you gain stormlight faster if you have this off...

Border

You know that big red rectangle arround the perrimeter of the map? Don't step on it. Really, I am not sure why you would want to. It will kill you. I am very sorry for the inconvenience.

Terrirories

You lose health if you enter someone elses territory, and gain strength if you visit yours.
